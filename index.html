The "Ey' Bro" Legacy

Twenty years ago, a clip of Eminem and his friends hilariously mispronouncing Åbro beer as "Ey Bro" went viral, amassing over a million views.
Embracing the joke, 20 years later Åbro released a special edition beer named "Ey Bro," which quickly became a hit.
Now, we've created the "Ey Bro" meme coin to honor this legendary internet moment and the beloved beer it inspired. Join us in celebrating this unique blend of humor and hops!
